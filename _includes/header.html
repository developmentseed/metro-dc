DC's NON-GEOGRAPHICAL

MONOSPACED

METRO

DC's Non-Geographical Monospaced Metro is a project with the objective of exploring unconventional visual representations of ordinary data sets. Maps are successful organizations of data, and although very useful, represent only one way of combining information. The goal here is to organize, explore and transform common data sets in interesting visual fashions that tell different stories.
