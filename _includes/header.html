DC's NON-GEOGRAPHICAL

MONOSPACED

METRO

DC's Non-Geographical Monospaced Metro is a project which's objective is the exploration of unconventional visuals for given data sets. A map is a common geographical organization and a very useful one. However, it's been done and won't be further explored here. The goal is to organize and explore ordinary data sets to create different stories and visualizations.
